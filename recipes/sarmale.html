Sarmale

Description

This recipe is a very traditional Moldavian recipe. My mother and my grandmother used to cook it at almost every Moldavian holy day and sometimes on a casual day. I love it! If you cannot find parsley root, you may substitute parsnip or turnip.

Ingredients :

  • 3 ¼ cups long grain rice, rinsed
  • 2 pounds pork loin roast, finely diced
  • 1 pound carrots, chopped
  • 1 pound onions, chopped
  • 1 pinch salt to taste
  • 3 tablespoons tomato paste
  • ½ teaspoon dried dill weed
  • ¼ cup sunflower seed oil
  • 6 ounces parsley roots, chopped
  • 1 medium head cabbage
